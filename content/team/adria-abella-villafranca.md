## Bio

Adria Abella Villafranca is a mechanical engineer and the technical lead behind SchoolMe's AI-powered medical education platform. Before moving into full-time startup leadership, Adria spent six years as Data Warehouse Administrator at the [UAMS Institute for Digital Health & Innovation](https://idhi.uams.edu/), where he built and maintained the research data infrastructure that powered dozens of peer-reviewed studies and multiple patents in waveform analysis, surgical informatics, and medical device development.

He now serves as a Researcher in the Department of Surgery at VUMC, supporting translational informatics and medical device programs alongside his role as CTO of SchoolMe.
