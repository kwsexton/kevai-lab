## What We Do

Medical AI is easy to demo and hard to trust. The KEV.AI Lab works the gap in between.

We acquire expert clinical knowledge, represent it in ways machines can reason over, and validate that the systems we build preserve what the experts meant. That work spans two frontiers:

- **Medical education** — AI tutors, simulators, and adaptive learning systems that transmit clinical expertise at scale.
- **Clinical knowledge extraction** — validated AI pipelines that turn unstructured electronic health record data into research-grade evidence.

Across both, the commitment is the same: AI systems for medicine must be rigorous, auditable, and worthy of the patients and learners who depend on them.

---

## A Lab of Builders

KEV.AI is not a typical academic lab. Every member is a working clinician, engineer, or operator who has **shipped products**, **founded companies**, or **built enterprise systems** in healthcare. We believe the sharpest insights about AI in medicine come from contact with real users, real data, and real regulatory environments — not from theory alone.
